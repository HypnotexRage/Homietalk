The Truth About Depression

Depression is something that you cannot control. It has a tendency to take over how you feel and it almost makes you feel like a hostage in your own body. It can feel like a constant battle ⚔. It can really feel like it's dragging you down ⚓

Depression Symptoms

Depression has behavioral symptoms such as:

  • agitation
  • excessive crying,
  • irritability
  • restlessness
  • social isolation
